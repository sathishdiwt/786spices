FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

✦ ❖ ✦
Where is 786 Spices Bazaar located in Singapore?
786 Spices Bazaar has 3 mini mart outlets: Kaki Bukit (18 Kaki Bukit Rd 3 #01-13, S415978), Neythal Road (20 Neythal Rd, S628585), and Tuas View Square (36 Tuas View Square #01-02B, S637613). Food stalls operate at Neythal Road and Jalan Papan Avery Lodge.
Does 786 Spices sell Bangladeshi groceries and masala in Singapore?
Yes. 786 Spices Bazaar specialises in Bangladeshi groceries, masala, spices, halal meat, Bangladeshi snacks and everyday essentials. All products are halal and sourced to serve the South Asian and Bangladeshi community working and living in Singapore.
How can I pay at 786 Spices Bazaar?
We accept Cash, PayNow (UEN: 202428421E or 202113214E), NETS, and CC card terminals at select outlets. Scan our PayNow QR code with any Singapore banking app for instant payment.
Can I order from 786 Spices via WhatsApp?
Yes! WhatsApp us at +65 8 9009 687 (Neythal Rd) or +65 80 474 786 (Tuas) or +65 81 544 644 (Kaki Bukit) to enquire about product availability, place bulk orders, or get directions to the nearest outlet.
Is 786 Spices Bazaar halal?
786 Spices Bazaar is Muslim-owned and operated. All meat, poultry and food products are halal. The name "786" is the numerical value of Bismillah ir-Rahman ir-Rahim in Islamic tradition, representing our values of faith and integrity in business.
What products do you sell at 786 Spices?
Our outlets stock a wide range including: Bangladeshi masala and spices, halal fresh and frozen meat, rice, lentils (dal), Bangladeshi snacks and sweets, cooking oil, condiments, beverages, household essentials, and personal care products — everything a Bangladeshi or South Asian household needs in Singapore.
